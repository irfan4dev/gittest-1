How Does Smartiks Do It?

Our team has built a proprietary smart contract and API that leverages the Ethereum blockchain to provide unparalleled control of how tickets are bought, resold, and transferred between fans. The average ticket for a live show changes hands multiple times before being scanned at the door creating layers between the entertainers and their fans. Every time a ticket moves, revenue, and data is lost while fans risk buying fraudulent and inflated tickets. Smart tickets, by design, allow the original issuers to decide how they can be bought, resold and transferred from one person or another. They also create an audit trail for each ticket that provides valuable pricing, customer insights, and a direct sales channel to capture more revenue from each fan.
